In the Beginning

Rent A Pet began with a family that loved dogs. George Smith and his wife Carol owned two Irish Setters. One day while walking his dogs, George noticed how much attention they attracted. A woman mentioned she wished she could have a dog but couldn’t due to her living situation. This sparked an idea—what if people could rent pets?

Advantages of the Business

  • Access to pets without long-term responsibility
  • Ability to choose preferred pets
  • Opportunities for exercise and companionship
  • No cost for upkeep or medical expenses

Challenges of the Business

  • Housing and feeding animals
  • Medical care expenses
  • Liability insurance requirements
  • Full-time responsibility for pet care

Where We Are Today

Rent A Pet officially began in 1980 with dog rentals. Since then, the company has expanded to include cats, horses, pigs, and even snakes. Customers can enjoy pet companionship without the responsibilities of ownership.

Return to Top

What Makes Us Unique

Rent A Pet has grown from Provo to multiple locations including Logan, Bountiful, Salt Lake City, and St. George. The company employs a veterinarian and a trainer to ensure all animals are safe and well cared for.

The company serves a wide variety of customers, including seniors in retirement homes, individuals seeking companionship, and even people looking for unique pets for events or entertainment.

Popular Uses

  • Companionship for seniors
  • Social interaction opportunities
  • Events, movies, and parties
  • Seasonal rentals like snakes for Halloween
